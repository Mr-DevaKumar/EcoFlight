Why Route Matters

Flight emissions vary significantly based on routing, aircraft type, and stopovers. Choosing smarter routes can reduce your carbon footprint by up to 30%.

Direct Flights

Takeoff and landing create the most emissions - direct flights minimize these.

Modern Aircraft

Newer planes like Boeing 787 are 20-25% more fuel efficient.

Optimal Altitude

Proper flight paths can reduce fuel consumption significantly.

Tips for Greener Flying

Fly Non-Stop

Choose direct flights when possible to avoid the high emissions from takeoffs and landings.

Daytime Flights

Daytime flights have less climate impact than nighttime ones due to contrail formation.

Choose Economy

Business class has 3x the footprint of economy due to space taken on the plane.

Newer Aircraft

Look for flights using Boeing 787 or Airbus A350 which are 20-25% more efficient.
